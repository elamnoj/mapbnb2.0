An Overview of Airbnb's in Boston

Where to find the most listings...

You'll find the most listings in the South End and East Boston. Surronding neighborhoods like the Back Bay, Beacon Hill, Downtown, and Chinatown also feature a high density of neighbothoods.

The Dorchester neighborhood offers a relatively high density of listings at modest prices.

Where you'll spend the most

You'll spend the most in the South Boston Waterfront averaging nearly $200 per night. Next, Downtown, Charlestown, and West Roxbury averages just over $200. Then, Back Bay , the Leather District, Fenway, and South Boston all average under $200.
