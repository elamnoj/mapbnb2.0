CHICAGO

An Overview of Airbnb's in Chicago

Where to find the most listings...

You'll find the most listings in the the Lakeview neighborhood with nearly 600 listings. Surronding neighborhoods like the Lincoln Park, and Uptown also feature a high density of neighborhoods.

But neighborhoods like Logan Square, West Town, and the Loop offer the next highest concentration of listings. Uptown or Logan Square may offer the best density to price offerings in the city.

×

Where you'll spend the most

You'll spend the most in the the Near North and South Sides, with the former averaging nearly $350 per night and the latter just over $300. Next, Lincoln Park averages just under $250. Then, Lakeview, the Loop, West Town, North Center, and Logan Square all average around $200.
